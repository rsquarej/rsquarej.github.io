Raghavender Jillela

I am a engineer at Apple in the Video Applications group.

I work on computer vision and machine learning for various video editing applications such as Final Cut Pro, iMovie, Motion, Clips, and a few other applications. Prior to joining Apple, I worked at two different startups focused on 3D LiDAR for face recognition and automotive applications. I have a PhD in EE, focused on CVML from West Virginia University.
